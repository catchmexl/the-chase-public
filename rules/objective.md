The **Escapers** try to get away, while the **Chasers** attempt to track them down within the defined area in the city.

**Chasers win** by locating the Escapers and getting close enough to clearly identify them, roughly within speaking distance. No physical contact needed.
**Escapers win** by surviving until the timer ends without being caught.

The game involves strategy, transportation management, and quick decision-making.

*Use the city's transport network, timing, and special actions. Outthink, don't outrun.*

> 🤝 The Chase runs on sportsmanship — if a Chaser is close enough to call you out, you're caught.
